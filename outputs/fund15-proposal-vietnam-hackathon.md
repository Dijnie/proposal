# PROJECT & SOLUTION

## [SOLUTION] Please describe your proposed solution

### How We Perceive the Problem

Cardano's global adoption depends on developer talent pipelines in emerging markets. Southeast Asia, particularly Vietnam, represents a massive untapped opportunity:

- **100,000+ computer science students** across Vietnamese universities (Ministry of Education data)
- **500,000+ software developers** in Vietnam's tech industry (TopDev, ITViec reports)
- **<0.1% Cardano awareness** among this population (our community surveys)
- **Zero large-scale Cardano hackathons** have been held in Vietnam to date

Meanwhile, Ethereum, Solana, and Polygon have all run major SEA hackathons, securing early mindshare among the region's developers. Cardano risks being perceived as "not active in Asia" if we don't establish presence.

The problem is threefold:
1. **Awareness Gap**: Vietnamese developers don't know what Cardano offers
2. **Education Gap**: No accessible Cardano training tailored for Vietnamese universities
3. **Activation Gap**: No flagship event to convert interest into on-chain building

### Why Our Approach

**University partnerships are the key to sustainable ecosystem growth.** Unlike public hackathons that attract transient participants, university-embedded events create long-term communities:

- **Curriculum Integration**: Pre-hackathon workshops (3-4 weeks) taught by Cardano mentors + embedded into university CS programs
- **Student Club Infrastructure**: Establish Cardano clubs at all 4 universities → monthly meetups, project incubation beyond event
- **Alumni Network**: Create multi-year relationship where 2026 participants mentor 2027 participants

**We've validated this model.** Our team successfully ran a previous blockchain hackathon with 50-100 participants and 10-20 completed projects. We know what works:
- Clear technical workshops before the event (prevents "I don't know where to start")
- Strong mentor-to-participant ratio (1 mentor per 8-10 teams)
- Multi-phase structure: training → building → pitching (not just a weekend sprint)
- Post-event support (helps teams continue beyond hackathon)

**Why Vietnam, why now:**
- **Tech Hub Momentum**: HCMC and Hanoi are top 10 fastest-growing tech ecosystems in Asia (Startup Genome)
- **Government Support**: Vietnam's National Digital Transformation Program prioritizes blockchain adoption
- **Cost Efficiency**: $30k USD (~60k ADA) delivers 200 participants and 10 on-chain projects - 10x more cost-effective than US/EU events
- **Regional Gateway**: Success in Vietnam enables expansion to Thailand, Philippines, Indonesia (we have contacts at universities in all 3)

### Who Will This Engage

**Primary Audience: 200 University Students**
- Computer Science, Software Engineering, and related majors
- 2nd-4th year students (have programming fundamentals, available for 6-week program)
- Recruited via university CS departments, tech clubs, and social media
- Mix of beginners (70%) and intermediate developers (30%) to ensure accessibility

**Secondary Audience: Cardano Developer Mentors**
- 5-8 experienced Cardano developers (Plutus, Aiken, Mesh, Lucid)
- Recruited via IOG Discord, Cardano Developer Portal, partnerships with Gimbalabs/Emurgo
- Provide technical workshops, code reviews, deployment guidance

**Tertiary Audience: Vietnamese Tech Ecosystem**

- Creates visibility for Cardano beyond just participants
