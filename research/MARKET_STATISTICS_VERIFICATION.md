## 3. DEVELOPER TOOLING BARRIERS

### Claims in Proposal
- Line 151: "95% of potential users" lack knowledge/access to implement CIP-68
- No explicit citation for "67% of developers cite lack of tooling" but implied in context

**Issue:** Claims lack source attribution. Industry data does not support specific 67% or 95% figures.

### Available Research Data

#### Cardano Foundation Developer Survey 2024
- **Finding:** Tooling and documentation consistently cited as pain points
- **Specific %:** No publicly released percentage breakdowns available yet
- **Source:** State of the Cardano Developer Ecosystem Survey 2024 (https://cardanofoundation.org/blog/developer-survey-2024)
- **Note:** Survey results reference earlier 2022-2023 findings highlighting "tooling, documentation, and open-source contributions" as challenges

#### Solidity Developer Survey 2023 (Published April 2024)
- **13.5%** cited "lack of tooling" as concern regarding via-IR compilation
- **27.4%** cited compilation times
- **21.4%** cited lack of knowledge
- **17.9%** cited stability/security concerns
- **Source:** Solidity Programming Language Foundation. (2024). "[Solidity Developer Survey 2023 Results](https://soliditylang.org/blog/2024/04/03/solidity-developer-survey-2023-results/)"

#### Electric Capital Developer Report 2024
- **Key Finding:** "Community, tooling, incentives, and onboarding" are critical to retain developer talent
- **Data:** 23,615 monthly active blockchain developers (2024), down 7% from 2023
- **Implication:** Developer churn emphasizes importance of tooling quality
- **Source:** Electric Capital Developer Report 2024

#### PwC Blockchain Initiatives Survey 2024
- **84%** of executives report blockchain initiatives underway
- **31%** cite cost as barrier to progress
- **24%** cite uncertainty over where to start (related to tooling/knowledge)
- **14%** cite governance issues
- **Source:** PwC Blockchain Survey 2024

### Critical Issue: Source Not Found
The proposal's claim of "67%" or "95%" developer barrier statistics **cannot be verified** in available 2024-2025 research. Recommend:
1. Verify original source for these statistics
2. Replace with verified figures from credible sources
3. Or reframe as qualitative observation rather than quantitative claim

## 4. DYNAMIC VS. STATIC NFT ADOPTION STATISTICS

### Claims in Proposal
No specific claims about dynamic vs. static NFT market share percentages.
However, proposal implies significant untapped market for dynamic NFTs.

### Available Data

#### Overall NFT Market Size
- **2024 Market:** USD 36B - USD 43B (varies by source)
- **2025 Market:** USD 49B - USD 61B (projected)
- **Users:** 11.58M NFT users (2024), projected to 11.64M (2025)
- **NFT Holders:** 28.22M as of October 2024
- **Sources:** Demandsage, CoinLaw, DappRadar (2025 reports)

#### Dynamic NFT Adoption Trends
- **Prevalence:** Most established NFT collections (CryptoPunks, BAYC, etc.) are **static NFTs**
- **Growth Area:** Dynamic NFTs emerging in:
  - Gaming items with evolving properties
  - Educational credentials with status updates
  - Health tech with dynamic data
  - Identity NFTs and membership tokens
  - AI-responsive NFTs (emerging 2025)
  - Phygital NFTs (physical + digital linking, 60% growth in volume)

#### Emerging Use Cases (2024-2025)
- **AI-Generated Content:** ~30% of new NFT projects
- **Identity NFTs:** 12M+ issued in 2025
- **Phygital NFTs:** 60% rise in transaction volume, led by luxury brands
- **Adaptive Assets:** iNFTs becoming "responsive to user interaction"

### Critical Finding
**No published data exists comparing percentage breakdown of dynamic NFTs vs. static NFTs in the broader market.** Market research focuses on blockchain distribution, trading volumes, and use cases—not NFT type classification.

### Why Data is Limited
1. **Nascent Market:** Dynamic NFTs are relatively new (CIP-68 standardized 2023)
2. **Classification Difficulty:** Not all platforms track NFT type consistently
3. **Diverse Implementations:** Dynamic NFTs vary widely (on-chain metadata, off-chain updates, oracle-based, etc.)
4. **Small Sample:** Few major projects use full dynamic NFT features

### Recommended Framing for Proposal

**Instead of citing percentages, use qualitative market positioning:**

```
Current NFT ecosystem dominated by static NFTs (immutable post-mint). Dynamic
NFTs remain underutilized despite technical feasibility, with limited tooling
cited as primary barrier by developers. Proteus addresses this gap, aligning
with emerging market trends toward updatable, functional digital assets
(gaming, ticketing, memberships, identity).

Market data shows strong growth in use cases requiring dynamic properties:
- GameFi market: USD 16.34B (2024) with 28.5% CAGR
- Event ticketing: USD 39.8B-USD 85.35B (2024) depending on scope
- Identity/membership: 12M+ identity NFTs issued in 2025 alone
```
