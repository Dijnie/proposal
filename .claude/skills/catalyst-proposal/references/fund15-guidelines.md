# Fund15 Guidelines

## Categories & Budget Ranges

### Cardano Partners (Tier-1 Enterprise)
- **Budget**: 250,000 - 750,000 ADA
- **Pool**: 10,000,000 ADA total
- **Focus**: R&D pilots with globally recognized enterprises
- **Requirement**: Evidence of enterprise partnership/collaboration
- **Impact**: Mainstream Cardano adoption, co-marketing opportunities

### Cardano Use Cases (Prototype & Launch)
- **Budget**: 15,000 - 200,000 ADA
- **Pool**: 6,000,000 ADA total
- **Focus**: On-chain prototypes/MVPs by technical teams
- **Requirement**: Prototype testable on-chain by project end
- **Impact**: New on-chain utility for Cardano

### Cardano Open (Ecosystem)
- **Budget**: 15,000 - 60,000 ADA
- **Pool**: 2,500,000 ADA total
- **Focus**: Non-technical ecosystem growth
- **Requirement**: Community engagement, education, research
- **Impact**: Grassroots growth and broader Cardano reach

## Character Limits

| Field | Limit |
|-------|-------|
| Title | 60 chars (recommended) |
| Problem Statement | 200 chars |
| Solution Overview | 200 chars |
| Solution Detail | 10,000 chars |
| Impact Section | 10,000 chars |
| Capability Section | 10,000 chars |

## Compliance Requirements

1. **Proposal Limit**: Max 2 proposals per participant
2. **Team Verification**: Verifiable LinkedIn/portfolio for ALL team members
3. **Prior Commitments**: Must be compliant with previous Catalyst projects
4. **Self-Assessment**: Mandatory checklist completion
5. **Platform**: Submit via Catalyst App only
6. **Proof of Life**: Verification before initial funding release
7. **Disclosure**: All participants must disclose roles across proposals

## Timeline Requirements
- Minimum: 2 months
- Maximum: 12 months
- Early completion allowed if milestones approved ahead of schedule
