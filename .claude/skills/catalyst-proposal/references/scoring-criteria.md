# Scoring Criteria

Community reviewers score proposals 1-5 on three criteria.

## Impact (Weight: ~35%)

**Question**: Does the proposal positively impact Cardano ecosystem?

### 4-5 Stars (HIGH)
- Clearly demonstrates and explains positive impact
- Impact supported with tangible, measurable evidence
- Realistic and achievable outcomes
- Proper community engagement and communication
- Answers: "By developing X we achieve A, B, C measured by 1, 2, 3"

### 3 Stars (MEDIUM)
- Partially demonstrated potential impact
- Gaps and omissions exist
- Proposed impact may not be achievable
- Doesn't clearly explain real difference

### 1-2 Stars (LOW)
- Failed to demonstrate ecosystem impact
- Impact not properly defined or elaborated
- Expected low impact

**Checklist for 4-5 Stars:**
- [ ] Clear positive ecosystem impact explanation
- [ ] Measurable evidence specified (e.g., "100 active users in month 6")
- [ ] Realistic, achievable outcomes
- [ ] Strong community engagement plan

## Feasibility (Weight: ~35%)

**Question**: Is project feasible? Does team have capability?

### 4-5 Stars (HIGH)
- Clearly feasible project
- Team demonstrates experience, capacity, skills
- Risks and mitigations understood and addressed
- Low risk of failure to complete

### 3 Stars (MEDIUM)
- Partially demonstrates feasibility
- Missing/unclear info about team, objectives, execution
- Some risk project may not complete successfully

### 1-2 Stars (LOW)
- Project not feasible
- High risk of failure to complete

**Checklist for 4-5 Stars:**
- [ ] Team demonstrates necessary skills with evidence
- [ ] Clear plan with easily understood outcomes
- [ ] Reasonable timelines and KPIs
- [ ] Risks identified with mitigation strategies
- [ ] Key milestones demonstrating feasibility

## Value for Money (Weight: ~30%)

**Question**: Is funding reasonable and good value for Treasury?

### 4-5 Stars (HIGH)
- Clearly demonstrates good Treasury value
- All financial aspects explained and defined
- Team capable of properly managing funds
- Specific, trackable milestones

### 3 Stars (MEDIUM)
- Partially demonstrates value
- Missing info on ADA spending
- Milestones too generic for progress tracking

### 1-2 Stars (LOW)
- Not good value for money

**Checklist for 4-5 Stars:**
- [ ] All funding amounts clearly explained and justified
- [ ] Team's financial management capability demonstrated
- [ ] Specific, non-generic milestones
- [ ] All costs calculated in ADA
- [ ] Resource gaps addressed (hiring plans)
- [ ] Competitive rates with supporting evidence

## Red Flags

**Impact:** Vague claims, benefits only proposer, non-existent problem, unrealistic projections
**Feasibility:** Anonymous team, ambitious timeline, vague technical details, no risk/prior work
**Value for Money:** Inflated budget, vague categories, no justification, no sustainability plan
