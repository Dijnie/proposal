### Phase 2: Detailed Analysis

**Analyze each of the three scored sections independently:**

#### A. IMPACT Analysis

**Questions to Consider:**
- Has proposal clearly demonstrated positive impact on Cardano ecosystem?
- Is impact properly defined and elaborated?
- Are there tangible, measurable evidence of impact?
- Is the impact realistic and achievable?
- Does team commit to proper community engagement and communication?

**Evidence Review:**
- [ ] Clear explanation of positive ecosystem impact
- [ ] Specific, measurable metrics defined (not vague)
- [ ] Realistic outcomes based on budget/timeline
- [ ] Community engagement plan exists
- [ ] Answers "by developing X we will achieve A, B, C measured by 1, 2, 3"

#### B. FEASIBILITY/CAPABILITY Analysis

**Questions to Consider:**
- Is this project feasible based on proposal submitted?
- Do plan, budget, and milestones look achievable?
- Does team have skills, experience, capability, and capacity?
- Is risk understanding demonstrated with mitigation strategies?
- Are timelines and KPIs reasonable?

**Evidence Review:**
- [ ] Team demonstrates necessary skills (verifiable LinkedIn, portfolio)
- [ ] Plan is easily understood with clear outcomes
- [ ] Milestones are specific and verifiable (not generic)
- [ ] Timeline is reasonable for complexity
- [ ] Risks identified with mitigation plans
- [ ] Low risk of failure to complete

#### C. VALUE FOR MONEY Analysis

**Questions to Consider:**
- Does funding request represent good value for treasury?
- Are all costs clearly explained and justified?
- Can team manage funds properly?
- Are milestones specific enough to track progress?
- Are costs calculated in ADA?
- Are resource gaps addressed?

**Evidence Review:**
- [ ] All funding amounts explained and justified
- [ ] Team financial management capability shown
- [ ] Milestones are specific, non-generic
- [ ] All costs in ADA (not USD or other currency)
- [ ] Hiring/resource gaps addressed if present
- [ ] Competitive rates with evidence

### Phase 3: Scoring Calculation

**Apply Official Scoring Rubric:**

**5 STARS - GREAT ANSWER:**
- Impact: Clearly demonstrates high positive impact with measurable evidence, realistic outcomes, strong community engagement
- Feasibility: Team clearly demonstrates capability, plan easily understood, reasonable timeline/KPIs, risks understood with mitigation
- Value: All costs justified, financial capability shown, specific milestones, competitive rates

**4 STARS - GOOD ANSWER:**
- Impact: Demonstrates good impact with some measurable evidence, mostly realistic, decent engagement plan
- Feasibility: Team shows capability with minor gaps, plan mostly clear, timeline mostly reasonable, some risk awareness
- Value: Most costs justified, some financial capability shown, mostly specific milestones, reasonable rates

**3 STARS - ACCEPTABLE ANSWER:**
- Impact: Partially demonstrates impact but with gaps, somewhat realistic, basic engagement plan
- Feasibility: Team has some capability but gaps exist, plan has unclear parts, timeline might be optimistic
- Value: Some costs justified but gaps exist, limited financial capability shown, some generic milestones

**2 STARS - POOR ANSWER:**
- Impact: Minimal impact demonstration, significant gaps, questionable realism, weak engagement
- Feasibility: Significant capability gaps, plan unclear, unrealistic timeline, limited risk awareness
- Value: Costs poorly justified, weak financial capability, mostly generic milestones, rates unclear

**1 STAR - VERY POOR ANSWER:**
- Impact: No clear impact, not defined/elaborated, unrealistic, no engagement plan
- Feasibility: High risk of failure, major capability/information gaps, plan not feasible
- Value: Costs unjustified, no financial capability shown, no specific milestones, poor value

### Phase 4: Detailed Feedback

**For each dimension, provide:**
1. Score (1-5 stars)
2. Detailed rationale (minimum 100 words)
3. Strengths identified
4. Weaknesses/gaps identified
5. Specific improvement suggestions
