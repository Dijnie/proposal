# Cardano Gaming Ecosystem Gap Analysis

**Research Date:** 2025-11-26

## Executive Summary

Cardano gaming ecosystem growing but concentrated on metaverses (Cornucopias, Pavia) and action games. **Critical gap: No casual/board games leveraging Hydra.** Infrastructure ready (SDKs, CIP-68, Hydra), but turn-based strategy games underrepresented.
