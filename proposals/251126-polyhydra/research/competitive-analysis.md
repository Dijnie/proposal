**Date:** 2025-11-26

## Executive Summary

Cardano GameFi is emerging but fragmented. Major projects (Cornucopias, Cardano Warriors) focus on complex metaverses requiring downloads. No competitor combines Hydra L2 + Discord distribution + provably-fair mechanics. This creates a clear differentiation opportunity for Polyhydra.

## 4. Success/Failure Patterns

### Why GameFi Projects Succeed
1. **Player-first design** - Fun before earning
2. **Sustainable tokenomics** - No hyperinflation
3. **Low friction onboarding** - Simple wallet setup
4. **Community engagement** - Active Discord/social
5. **Technical innovation** - Novel mechanics

### Why GameFi Projects Fail
1. **Poor gameplay** - Earning-only focus
2. **Economic collapse** - Token dumps
3. **Complex UX** - Web3 barriers
4. **Low quality** - Can't compete with traditional games
5. **Regulatory issues** - Unclear token status

### Cardano-Specific Challenges
- Lower TVL than competitors
- Smaller developer community
- Plutus learning curve
- Perception of slow development
