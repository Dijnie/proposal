- 240 students trained across 12 cohorts in 6 months
- Target of 20+ Testnet MVPs creates tangible on-chain output
- AI-assisted approach lowers barrier for non-technical students

## Overall Assessment

This proposal presents a well-structured approach to establishing Cardano presence in South Vietnam's university ecosystem. The combination of Whiteboard Cardano's educational content expertise and Hub Network's institutional access creates a credible execution pathway.

The dual-track approach (Eco for business students, Dev for technical students) with AI-assisted tools lowers barriers while maintaining quality through 96 hours of expert technical mentorship. The strategic timing with Vietnam's 2026 Fintech Sandbox creates regulatory relevance.

Key strengths include the detailed budget allocation, reusable open-source course materials, and leveraging Hub Network's planned 20+ events for free recruitment. The university partnerships with NTT (Catalyst-connected lecturer) and PTIT (Fintech Lab) provide credible academic anchors.

Concerns include the intensive 12-cohort schedule, dependency on informal university partnerships, and the minimal contingency allocation. The focus on Testnet MVPs rather than Mainnet deployment is realistic for a 6-month student program but limits immediate ecosystem impact.

Overall, this is a thoughtful proposal that addresses a real geographic gap in Cardano's academic presence while creating scalable educational infrastructure.
